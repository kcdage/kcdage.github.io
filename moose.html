MOOSE:Monitoring Observations Of SMC X-1's bEhaviour

Collaboration Overview & Goals

MOOSE is a monitoring program designed to study the soft X-ray fluctuations of SMC X-1 as it undergoes super-orbital period variability. SMC X-1 has previously been observed to undergo three epochs of super-orbital period variability. The fourth epoch began in 2020-2021, and is subsequently being monitored by NICER.

The physical mechanism for SMC X-1's superorbital periodicities is currently unclear; X-ray monitoring by MOOSE will help shed light on the nature of the source as the superorbital period varies.
