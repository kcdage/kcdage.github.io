I'm a third year graduate student of astrophysics and astronomy at Michigan State University. My advisor is Dr. Steve Zepf , and we study RZ 2109, an ultraluminous black hole in a globular cluster near NGC 4472. We do long-term monitoring in both x-ray and optical. I also am working on a project with Dr. Will Clarkson, searching for a spin-superorbital period relation in SMC X-1.
