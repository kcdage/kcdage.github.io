Mount Wilson Observatory

I stayed on the historic Mount Wilson Observatory for about two weeks in 2013 as a part of the CUREA (Consortium for Undergraduate Research and Education in Astronomy) program.

The observatory was founded in 1904 by George Ellery Hale and houses a 60-inch and 100-inch telescope, as well as two solar telescopes and the CHARA Array.
