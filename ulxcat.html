Catalogue of ultraluminous X-ray sources in extragalactic globular clusters

This table lists all known ultraluminous X-ray sources (with detected X-ray emission above \(10^{39}\) erg/s) in extragalactic globular clusters, along with their observed X-ray and optical properties.

Source Properties:
  • X-ray Spectral Shape: XX keV = characteristic inner disk temperature, XX = best fit power-law index
  • Behaviour: N=no statistically significant variability, T = transient, FV = variable (within an observation), LV=variable (over many years)
  • Optical Counterpart: C=spectroscopically confirmed, PC=photometric candidate
  • Optical Emission: ? = no constraints placed, ND=none detected above cluster continuum
  • Notes: U = ultracompact, R Cor Bor = R Corona Borealis Star, BH=black hole candidate, UCD=ultracompact dwarf galaxy
